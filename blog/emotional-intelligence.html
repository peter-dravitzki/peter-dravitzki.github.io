Emotional Intelligence

Describe the difference between EQ and IQ

IQ is a number used to express a person's overall mental ability, whereas EQ refers to a person's ability to perceive, control, evaluate and express emotions.

Describe why EQ is important and how it is relevent to you

Experts recognize that IQ is not the only determinant of life success. Life success depends on complex influences including EQ. You are more likely to be hired by companies if you work on your emotional intelligence. You will also be happier.
